## Content Production Standards

### Every Review Must Have
- [ ] Real current pricing (verified at vendor site)
- [ ] Rating breakdown by category (5 dimensions)
- [ ] Pros AND cons (minimum 4 each)
- [ ] FAQPage schema (minimum 4 FAQs)
- [ ] Review schema (JSON-LD)
- [ ] Affiliate link with nofollow + noopener
- [ ] Affiliate disclosure component
- [ ] CTA with free trial details
- [ ] Word count: 2,500–4,000 words

### Every Comparison Must Have
- [ ] Feature table (minimum 12 features)
- [ ] When to choose each (5+ criteria per tool)
- [ ] Price table with both annual and monthly
- [ ] FAQPage schema (minimum 3 FAQs)
- [ ] Our Verdict section
- [ ] Links to full individual reviews
- [ ] Word count: 1,500–2,500 words

### Every Blog Post Must Have
- [ ] Answer in first 150 words
- [ ] 5+ FAQs with schema
- [ ] Article schema (JSON-LD)
- [ ] Internal links to 3+ review pages
- [ ] At least 1 citation to authoritative external source (ABA, law journal, vendor)
- [ ] Word count: 1,500–2,500 words

1. ChatGPT: "What is the best legal practice management software?"
2. Perplexity: "Clio vs MyCase which is better?"
3. Claude: "How much does legal practice management software cost?"
4. Gemini: "Best solo attorney practice management software"

Track: Is CounselStack cited? Which page is cited? Which competitors are cited?

Use results to improve the pages that should be cited but aren't.
